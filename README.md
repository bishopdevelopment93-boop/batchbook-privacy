# Privacy Policy for BatchBook

**Last updated: June 25, 2026**

## Overview

BatchBook is a local-first business management app for cottage food businesses,
home bakers, and small-scale makers. We built it with a simple privacy principle:
your data belongs to you. It is stored on your device, and any cloud backup goes
directly to your own cloud storage — never to our servers.

## Data Collection

BatchBook does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.
We have no servers, no accounts, and no analytics. We do not know who you are,
what you sell, or who your customers are.

## Data Storage

All data you enter into BatchBook — including customer names, phone numbers,
order details, product information, and financial records — is stored exclusively
on your device using a local database.

## Backup

BatchBook includes an optional backup feature. If you choose to use cloud backup,
your data is written directly to the cloud storage destination you select.
BatchBook does not have access to your cloud storage — the transfer happens
entirely between your device and your personal account. We never see, access,
or store your backup files.

If you use the manual export feature, your backup file is saved to a location
you choose on your device. We have no access to that file.

## Third-Party Services

BatchBook uses Google Play Billing to process the optional one-time Pro upgrade
purchase. This transaction is handled entirely by Google. BatchBook does not
receive or store your payment information. Please refer to Google's Privacy
Policy for information on how Google handles payment data.

## Push Notifications

BatchBook's push notifications are generated entirely on your device based on
dates you have entered. No data is sent to any server to generate or deliver
these notifications.

## Children's Privacy

BatchBook is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not
knowingly collect any information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top
of this page. We will never introduce data collection without clearly disclosing
it here.
